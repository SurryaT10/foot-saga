Business Rules

1) A USER can create many ARTICLES. However, each ARTICLE can be created by exactly one USER. A USER may or may not create ARTICLES.
2) A USER can write many COMMENTS. However, each COMMENT can be written by exactly one USER. A USER may or may not write COMMENTS.
3) A COMMENT is associated with exactly one ARTICLE. However, an article includes many COMMENTS. An ARTICLE does not neccessarily require to have COMMENTS.
4) An ARTICLE belongs to exactly one CATEGORY. However, a CATEGORY can contain many ARTICLES. A CATEGORY does not neccessarily require to have ARTICLES.
